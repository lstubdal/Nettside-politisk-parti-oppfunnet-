About the party

The climate and nature crisis is destroying the sea and is the biggest the injustice of our time. Short-term populism, growing differences and environmental damage threaten freedom and the security many of us have taken for granted.

The antidote is green politics - and green politicians, like understand that a better world is possible. It requires us to be vigilant to think new, listen to professional knowledge and collaborate on across borders.

In the Mermaid Party, our starting point is that humans are not above nature and animals, but that we are part of it. We have three important principles of solidarity for all our policies: solidarity with other people future generations, and animals and nature.

We are part of an international, green grassroots movement with a global perspective. Social justice for all, regardless of class, orientation or skin color are key values.
